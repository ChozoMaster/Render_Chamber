Job Experiences

RESEARCH ASSISTANT

Fraunhofer Institute for Computer Graphics Research

During my studies I worked as an research assistant at Fraunhofer IGD in Darmstadt. I started in the department for Interactive Engineering Technologies and worked on a low cost but high precision 3D scanner, called Meso Scanner. My main occupation was the quality optimization of the result and the development of an intuitive GUI.
In 2013 a new department emerged from our group which is called Cultural Heritage Digitization. Henceforth I was responsible for the development of the second version of the Meso Scanner, which features high performance industrial cameras and an overall more sophisticated setup. My tasks were camera calibration, GUI design, image processing and GPGPU programming to speed up the reconstruction algorithms. Additionally I supported the development of utility libraries for our department.
